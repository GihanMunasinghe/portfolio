About

Engineer by craft, teacher at heart

I'm Gihan Munasinghe, a Singapore-based engineering lead with 8+ years designing, building and operating cloud-native, high-availability backend systems. Today I lead engineering at a major bank in Singapore, driving legacy-to-microservices migrations and setting engineering standards adopted across teams.

Along the way I've shipped software for banking (treasury and client platforms), telecom (millions-of-users self-care apps) and e-commerce — across Java 7–21, the Spring ecosystem, Kafka, and AWS, GCP, Kubernetes and OpenShift.

That production-hardened experience is what I bring to my own apps, my consulting, and the coaching I offer to individuals and small groups. If you're building something — or want to learn how — I'd love to hear from you.
